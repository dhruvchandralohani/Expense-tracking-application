Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect information that you provide directly to us. This includes account information such as your email address and password, as well as expense data that you enter including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We may also collect usage information about how you interact with our service.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services. Specifically, we use your data to display your expense history, generate spending summaries and insights, personalize your experience, and communicate with you about service updates. We do not sell your personal or financial data to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers with industry-standard encryption and security measures. We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us or deleting your account through the application settings.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal data with third parties except as required by law or with your explicit consent. We may use trusted third-party service providers to host our infrastructure, process payments, or send communications on our behalf. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we specify.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can view and edit most of your information directly within the application. You may also export your data at any time for your own records or to transfer to another service.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through the application. We are committed to addressing your concerns and ensuring your data is handled responsibly.
